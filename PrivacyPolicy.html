Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Secure Notes ("the App") is designed to help users create, store, and manage personal notes securely. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Secure Notes does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with the developer or third parties.

The notes, passwords, and other content you create within the App are stored locally on your device. The developer does not have access to this information.

Data Storage

All user-generated content is stored locally on your device. The App does not operate any backend servers for storing user notes or personal information.

Internet Access

The App does not require an account and does not transmit your notes to the developer. If future versions introduce online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Services

Secure Notes does not use third-party advertising networks.

If analytics, crash reporting, or other third-party services are added in future releases, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

The App is designed to help protect your notes and personal information through local storage and security features provided within the application. However, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Rights

Because data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your notes and App data at any time by using the App's deletion features or uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
